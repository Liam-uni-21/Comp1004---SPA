Welcome

How to use flashcards:


1. Select the flashcard set from the flashcards home menu
2. Try your best to answer the question on the front of the card
3. Flip the card by clicking on the flashcard in the center
4. Whether you knew the answer click don't know or do know
5. Repeat this until you're taken back to the flashcard home
How to create flashcards:

1. Select Create new flashcard set on the flashcards home menu
2. Enter the question and answer you want to set to the flashcard
3. Now click on Create new card and enter your next question/answer
4. If you decide you want to remove or edit a card you can navigate prev and next and click the button or edit
5. Now that you're happy with your set you click on Save set and it will appear on the flashcards home
How to review your study:


1. Click Dashboard on the navigation bar to your left
2. If you have completed a task such as a quiz or flashcards there will be buttons there for you
3. Choose which task you want to review by clicking the button and there will be your incorrect answers
4. If you wish to retake a quiz or flashcards there is a button at the bottom for you to do so
